## What Is HackForge?

An enterprise hackathon management platform that lets organizations create, run, and manage hackathons end-to-end — from registration to winner declaration.

## Why Does It Exist?

Existing platforms (HackerEarth, Devpost, Unstop) charge INR 1.5L+ per event for limited users, offer rigid workflows, and force vendor dependency. HackForge offers a 10x cheaper, self-serve, configurable alternative.

## Who Is Building It?

Burhanuddin C. — solo developer, building this as a personal product with InMobi as the founding customer.

## Who Is It For?

Engineering and Innovation leaders at enterprises (50-50,000+ employees) who run internal hackathons 2-6 times per year. Secondary: HR/Talent teams, developer community managers, startups. Can be extended to college usecases.

## Business Model

Free for InMobi (founding customer, lifetime Pro access). SaaS subscription for other organizations: Free, Starter ($49/mo), Pro ($149/mo), Enterprise (custom).

## Current Stage

V1 MVP — 20 dev-day sprint. Goal: host InMobi's next internal hackathon end-to-end.
